A New Computing Paradigm

We are not just optimizing Current chips; we are rewriting the physical layer of data transmission.

Current high-performance computing (HPC) clusters rely on copper-based interconnects, which suffer from massive **heat generation** and **bandwidth degradation** at high frequencies. This is the "Thermal Wall."

HOCS Integrated solves this by seamlessly integrating high-speed **optical data paths** (silicon photonics) directly into the computing architecture. Our proprietary hybrid E/O conversion engine translates electrical processing data into optical pulses right at the chip's edge.

Designed for data centers and large-scale parallel processing required by LLMs and real-time AI inference, HOCS provides the necessary **hardware backbone** needed to push past the limits of traditional copper trace technology. The future of computing isn't electrical; it's hybrid.
