Breaking the
Thermal Wall.

As Large Language Models (LLMs) scale to trillions of parameters, the data centers powering them are hitting a physical barrier. Traditional copper traces generate massive heat at high frequencies, requiring unsustainable cooling infrastructure and capping system performance.

HOCS Integrated provides the escape velocity. By converting electrical signals into optical pulses directly at the processing edge (FPGA/SoM), we transmit massive data payloads through silicon waveguides. The result? Unprecedented bandwidth with near-zero heat dissipation along the data path.
