Optical Routing on Silicon

Current High-Performance Computing (HPC) clusters rely on electrical signaling, which suffers from severe signal degradation and massive thermal output at high frequencies. HOCS Integrated converts electrical signals into optical pulses right at the edge of the processor.

►

E/O Conversion Engine

Proprietary micro-lasers and modulators translate processing data into optical streams instantly.

►

Photonic Waveguides

Data travels through microscopic glass/silicon channels, completely immune to electromagnetic interference.

►

O/E Receiver Arrays

Ultra-fast photodetectors catch the light and convert it back to electrical signals for the compute core with near-zero latency.
